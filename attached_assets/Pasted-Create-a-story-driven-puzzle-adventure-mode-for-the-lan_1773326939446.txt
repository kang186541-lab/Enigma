Create a story-driven puzzle adventure mode for the language learning app.

The experience should feel like a mystery visual novel game similar to a puzzle detective adventure.

Main character:
Lingo the Fox – a clever language detective professor.

Player role:
Apprentice Linguist.

The story revolves around a secret organization manipulating languages around the world.

Title of the main storyline:
"The Language Conspiracy"

----------------------------------------------------

STORY STYLE

• Mystery detective atmosphere  
• Short cinematic dialogue scenes  
• Dramatic descriptions in parentheses  

Examples:

"(안개 낀 런던 거리… 가스등이 희미하게 빛난다…)"

"(긴장감 있는 음악이 흐른다…)"

Dialogue format:

Character Name  
Dialogue text

Scenes should feel like a visual novel.

----------------------------------------------------

STORY STRUCTURE

Each chapter contains:

• 8–10 story scenes
• 4–5 language puzzles
• clue discoveries
• chapter ending reward

Game flow:

Story Scene  
→ Puzzle  
→ Clue discovered  
→ Story continues

----------------------------------------------------

MAIN STORY

Languages around the world are changing mysteriously.

Words are disappearing.
Meanings are being altered.

A secret organization called "Lexicon Society" is manipulating languages.

Lingo the Fox and the player must travel around the world solving language puzzles to uncover the truth.

----------------------------------------------------
